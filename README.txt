This is an Eagle Library with the SNES and GameCube MultiAV Socket.

N64 will be added soon.
NES FRA RGB OUT will be added soon.



Pin3 is different on SNES/GC and N64

SNES & GC PinOUT

Pin 	Name 	Description
1 	R 	Red
2 	G 	Green
3 	+12V	+12 VDC 	<--!!
4 	B 	Blue
5 	GND 	Ground
6 	GND 	Ground
7 	Y 	S-Video Y (Luminance)
8 	C 	S-Video C (Chrominance)
9 	CVBS 	Composite Video
10 	+5V 	+5 VDC
11 	L+R 	Left+Right Audio (Mono)
12 	L-R 	Left-Right Audio (Used to calculate Stereo)


N64 PinOUT

Pin 	Name 	Description
1 	R 	Red
2 	G 	Green
3 	CSYNC 	Composite Sync	<--!!
4 	B 	Blue
5 	GND 	Ground
6 	GND 	Ground
7 	Y 	S-Video Y (Luminance)
8 	C 	S-Video C (Chrominance)
9 	CVBS 	Composite Video
10 	+5V 	+5 VDC
11 	L+R 	Left+Right Audio (Mono)
12 	L-R 	Left-Right Audio (Used to calculate Stereo)
